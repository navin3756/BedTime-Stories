# Sweetdreams Bedtime Stories Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Sweetdreams Bedtime Stories helps families create and listen to calming bedtime stories.

## Information the app may use

The app may let you enter optional bedtime preferences, such as a child's first name, age range, comfort focus, story idea, and favorite companion. These preferences are used to personalize stories.

The app may let you record a voice sample for Family Voice Studio. Voice recording is optional and requires consent in the app before a family voice can be created.

## Local storage

Sweetdreams stores saved stories, bedtime preferences, and saved family voice profile references on the device using local app storage. This helps the app remember recent stories and selected settings.

## Microphone

The app requests microphone access only for features that record or capture audio, such as Family Voice Studio or speaking a story idea. You can deny microphone access and still use typed story ideas and read stories on screen.

## Third-party services

Story generation is local-first. If a local model is unavailable, the app can use built-in story templates.

Family Voice Studio requires a configured voice provider service. When enabled, a consented voice sample and story text may be sent to the configured voice provider to create a private family voice and narration audio. Do not record another person's voice unless you have explicit permission.

## Children's privacy

Sweetdreams is designed for family bedtime use. Parents or guardians should supervise use by children. Do not enter unnecessary personal information about a child.

## Data sharing

Sweetdreams does not sell personal information. The app only sends data to a third-party voice provider when optional Family Voice Studio features are configured and used.

## Data deletion

You can remove saved stories and family voice references from inside the app. You can also clear app storage from Android settings to remove locally stored app data.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app publisher listed in the Google Play Store listing.
