# Sweetdreams Store Listing Draft

## App name

Sweetdreams Bedtime Stories

## Short description

Gentle bedtime stories, calming narration, and cozy sleep sounds for kids.

## Full description

Sweetdreams helps families create calming bedtime stories with fewer steps at night. Kids can pick a cozy pre-made story idea, or parents can type a custom idea and let the app turn it into a gentle story for sleep.

Features:

- One-tap bedtime story ideas for sleepy nights.
- Custom story prompts for a child's favorite worlds and companions.
- Soft read-aloud narration tuned for bedtime.
- Manual background music and sleep sounds.
- Sleep timer and calm wind-down routine options.
- Private on-device story composition with no account or API key.

Sweetdreams is designed for peaceful bedtime routines. Stories avoid scary conflict and focus on comfort, imagination, reassurance, and rest.
