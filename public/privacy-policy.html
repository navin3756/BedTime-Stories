Sweetdreams Bedtime Stories Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Sweetdreams Bedtime Stories helps families create and listen to calming bedtime stories.

Information the app may use

The app may let you enter optional bedtime preferences, such as a child's first name, age range, comfort focus, story idea, and favorite companion. These preferences are used only to personalize stories on the device.

Local storage

Sweetdreams stores saved stories and bedtime preferences on the device using local app storage. This helps the app remember recent stories and selected settings.

Story generation and narration

Story generation is local-first. If a local model is unavailable, Sweetdreams uses built-in story templates. Read-aloud narration uses voices available on the device. Sweetdreams does not send story text, preferences, or audio recordings to the developer or a third-party voice provider.

Children's privacy

Sweetdreams is designed for family bedtime use. Parents or guardians should supervise use by children. Do not enter unnecessary personal information about a child.

Data sharing

Sweetdreams does not sell, collect, or share personal information.

Data deletion

You can remove saved stories from inside the app. You can also clear app storage from Android settings to remove locally stored app data.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@abcdschoolofbollywood.com.
